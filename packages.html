Partnership FAQ

Clear expectations on how we move as one unit.

How does the partnership commitment work?
We don't believe in locking you into restrictive 12-month contracts, but we do believe in momentum. Most viral loops take 60-90 days of consistent engineering to reach peak velocity. We partner on a month-to-month basis, focusing on continuous ROI and growth. If we aren't delivering, you aren't trapped.
How is growth capital (Ad Spend) handled?
Your investment in the partnership covers our strategic engineering, creative direction, and media production. Ad spend is the external fuel we use to scale that production. We will work with you to determine the exact media budget needed, and that is paid directly to the platforms (Meta/Google).
Why "Partner" instead of "Hire"?
Hiring implies you give us tasks. Partnering implies we give you results. We don't just "do your social media"—we embed our expertise into your operations. We challenge your ideas, push the creative boundaries, and ensure we both win by dominating your specific market.
